Pizza

Finished pepperoni pizza ready to eat. This recipe is great for a family or friends dinner night .

Ingredients

  • flour
  • salt
  • tomatoes
  • mozzarella cheese
  • condiments of choice
  • toppings of choice

Steps

  1. mix the flour and salt until a dough is formed
  2. process the tomatoes and condiment together to form a sauce
  3. flatten the dough into a circular shape
  4. spread the sauce across the dough
  5. cut the cheese into small pieces and spread over the sauce
  6. add condiment and topping to taste
  7. bake in the oven at max temp for 10 minutes
  8. enjoy!
